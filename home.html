Frequently Asked Questions

1. How can I start learning English on this website?
You can start by exploring our beginner lessons, interactive exercises, and quizzes. We also offer structured courses to guide you step by step.
2. Is this website free to use?
Yes, all basic lessons and vocabulary features are completely free to use.
3. Do I need to create an account?
No, you can browse lessons without an account, but creating one helps track your progress.
4. How can I build my English vocabulary?
Practice regularly, learn new words daily, and use the pronunciation and meaning features available on this website.
5. Do you offer certificates for completed courses?
Currently, certificates are not available, but we plan to add them in future updates.
